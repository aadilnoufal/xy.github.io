ABOUT US

Founder

Diya Noble, a high school student, founded the organization in 2024. She is accomplished in Public Speaking, Debating, Writing, and has received more than 47 certificates and medals.

How We Started

Diya, inspired by her mother Lincy Noble, aims to provide a platform for individuals in their young age of 10 and 30s and beyond to freely express themselves, viewing talking as a superpower that should be accessible to all regardless of age.

Our Mission

Folks aged 10 and up, whether you're in your 30s, 40s, 50s, or beyond, with a burning passion for public speaking and debating, this is your ticket to join the fun!
